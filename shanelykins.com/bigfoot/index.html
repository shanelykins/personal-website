Some backstory

PG stated in his epic post "how to start a startup" that founders should aim to solve a problem that they've experienced first hand. If you can solve your own problem, then look for others that are experiencing the same problem and maybe there's an opportunity to build a business around it. Bigfoot was in fact a problem that I experienced many times. When traveling to a new place, you often want to plug yourself into the local ecosystem and experience the great local things.
